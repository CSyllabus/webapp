Why Is It Special

Works wonders on slim datasets!

All we need is a short course descriptions text to compare them.

Our application uses Natural Language Processing method called Latent Semantic Analysis to compare courses using the dataset of course descriptions. Common vector space representations suffer when coping with two classical natural language problems: synonymy and polysemy. First being not taking into consideration that two different words might have the same meaning to humans and second being the opposite - two same words having different meanings.

LSA uses co-occurrence of terms in these documents to alleviate this problem and capture the latent semantic association between them.

Motivation

Course Explorer

Csyllabus is part of projects proposed in the SCORE competition 2018 edition. Contest aims at promoting and fostering software engineering in Universities worldwide. Student teams from all over the world participate in a competition for students from undergraduate to master's level.

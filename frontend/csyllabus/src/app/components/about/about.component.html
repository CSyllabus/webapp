Our Best Propositions for You!

CSyllabus is imagined as a web platform which should ease up process of finding and comparing courses on domestic and foreign faculties. It will enable users to discover and compare courses on interactive way through web application. This “one click” app will save time and provide very useful information to interested parties.

Start exploring and discovering courses

Motivation

Course Explorer

Every year Erasmus student exchange tender offer opens and every year we face the same problems. How to find course that fits your education path right? Csyllabus app completly solves this problem with faculties providing data to us!

SCORE Contest

Csyllabus is part of projects proposed in the SCORE competition 2018 edition. Contest aims at promoting and fostering software engineering in Universities worldwide. Student teams from all over the world participate in a competition for students from undergraduate to master's level.

Course Comparator

Remember going to your desired University fo syllabus hunt? No more! Now, you select your domestic courses, select University you want to go and that's it! Information you are looking for is now two clicks away.

Team Work

This application is built by distributed team from Croatia and Italy. It is an example what you can achieve even if you are kilometers apart.

Distributed software development

Csyllabus is backed up by DSD course which student can enrole in Croatia, Italy and Sweden. Course gives students an insight in complexity of distributed / global software development.

Documentation and sustainability

Csyllabus app is well documented by all modern software developments standards. Also this app is not one time project. It will be the backbone of all future students exchanges.
